Privacy Policy

App Name: Bible Study · Effective Date: 2026-03-27

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in Bible Study (the "App"). The App is a local-first app that can be used without account sign-up, and some features (ads, external scripture/audio lookup) communicate with external services.

1. Information Collected/Stored

  • App settings: language, translation version, denomination, audio settings, notification settings
  • Study records: bookmarks, highlights, notes, recent reading/playback position
  • Local cache: temporary scripture/audio cache files
  • Purchase status: ad-removal product purchase state (store processing result)

Most of the information above is stored on the device (for example, SharedPreferences and app-specific storage).

2. External Service Integrations

  • External API communication for scripture text/audio lookup
  • Google AdMob (ad delivery)
  • In-app purchase through app stores (ad-removal product)

3. Ads and Consent (UMP)

The App uses Google AdMob SDK to provide ads. Depending on region and legal requirements, consent notices for ads/privacy processing may be shown through Google UMP (User Messaging Platform).

  • The app may check consent status at startup and show a consent form when required.
  • Users can access privacy options (reset/change consent) from the settings screen.
  • Ad personalization is handled based on user choice and applicable law.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The app may transmit necessary information to service providers within the scope of ad delivery, payment processing, and content delivery. Outside of those purposes, personal information is not sold arbitrarily.

5. Retention and Deletion

  • Locally stored data may be deleted when the app is uninstalled or app data is reset.
  • Cached data may be updated/cleared according to app operation policies.

6. User Rights

  • Delete data by uninstalling the app or resetting app settings/data
  • Revoke notifications/permissions
  • Submit inquiries regarding privacy handling

7. Children's Privacy

The app may be used by users including children, but it does not provide sign-up features intended to intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

8. Policy Changes

This Policy may be updated when laws, service features, or connected SDK policies change. Material changes will be announced through app update notes or related notices.
