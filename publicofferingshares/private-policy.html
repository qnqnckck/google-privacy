Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-24

This Privacy Policy describes how Public Offering Shares handles information. Public Offering Shares is an app that shows public offering share information, provides a calendar widget, and offers same-day alerts for relevant schedules.

1. Developer and app identity

App name: Public Offering Shares
Project name: publicofferingshares

2. What the app does

The app is intended to provide:

  • public offering share related data and schedule information

According to the current app description provided by the publisher, Public Offering Shares does not separately collect personal data from users for its own analytics, advertising, or profiling purposes.

Public Offering Shares does not sell user personal information. Public Offering Shares does not share personal information with advertisers or data brokers for advertising purposes.

5. Public market data and app content

The app may display public offering related information, schedules, or calendar data to users. This type of content is intended to help users view market schedules and upcoming dates inside the app and widget.

The app may provide same-day alerts and a widget-based calendar display. Depending on the device platform, notification or widget-related system capabilities may be used so the app can show reminders or schedule information to the user.

Based on the current description provided for this app, the app does not separately collect user data for third-party analytics, advertising, or behavioral profiling.
