Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-24

This Privacy Policy describes how Plant Watering Manager handles information. Plant Watering Manager is a local-first app designed to help users track plant watering schedules and watering history on their own device.

1. Developer and app identity

App name: Plant Watering Manager
Android application ID: com.localfirst.plant_watering_manager
iOS bundle ID: com.localfirst.plantWateringManager

2. What information the app uses

The app allows users to enter and manage plant care information, including:

  • plant name
  • room or location label entered by the user
  • watering interval
  • last watered date and time
  • notes entered by the user
  • archive status
  • watering history records, including plant name and watering timestamp

3. How information is stored

Plant Watering Manager stores the above information locally on the user's device using on-device app storage. The app is designed to work offline and does not require an account to use its core features.

4. Data collection and sharing

Plant Watering Manager does not send plant records or watering history to the developer's servers. Plant Watering Manager does not sell personal information. Plant Watering Manager does not share user-entered plant data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Analytics, advertising, and third-party services

Plant Watering Manager does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cloud sync services in its verified current implementation.

6. Permissions and device access

The released app does not request dangerous runtime permissions such as camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photos access. Development-only build variants may include internet access for Flutter tooling, but the app's intended user functionality is local-first.

7. Data retention

User-entered information remains on the device until the user edits or deletes it, clears app data, or uninstalls the app. Because the app does not rely on a developer-hosted account system for core storage, there is no server-side retention of plant records in the verified current implementation.

8. Data deletion

Users can reduce or remove stored data by:

  • editing or deleting plant entries inside the app where supported
  • clearing the app's local data through the device settings
  • uninstalling the app from the device

9. Security

The app follows a local-first design and avoids transmitting plant records to a remote backend in the verified current implementation. No software can guarantee absolute security, but limiting storage to the local device reduces unnecessary data transfer exposure.

10. Children's privacy

Plant Watering Manager is a general productivity and utility app. It does not intentionally use the app to collect personal information from children through a remote service in the verified current implementation. If store settings later target children under 13, this policy should be reviewed again alongside Google Play Families requirements.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app behavior changes. Any future version should keep the disclosures aligned with the app's actual released behavior and Google Play requirements.
